Refund and Cancellation Policy

At DropConnect, we aim to ensure complete customer satisfaction with our digital clipboard services.

1. 7-Day Free Trial

All new subscription plans include a 7-day free trial. You may cancel your subscription at any time during the 7-day trial period without incurring any charges.

2. Cancellation Policy

You can cancel your recurring subscription at any time via your Account Settings page or by contacting support. Once canceled, your premium features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

3. Refund Policy

Since digital access is delivered immediately upon payment verification via Cashfree, subscription payments are generally non-refundable. However, if you experience technical issues or accidental duplicate charges, please reach out within 7 days for a full refund review.

4. Processing Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed back to your original payment method via Cashfree within 5–7 business days.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about refunds or cancellations, please contact us at support@dropconnect.com.
