Privacy Policy

Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy explains how DropConnect collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our universal clipboard service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal essential information required to provide our service, such as your email address, authentication credentials (Google Sign-In), and account session tokens.

2. End-to-End Encryption & Security

All temporary clipboard data and live stream transmissions are encrypted using client-side AES encryption. We do not inspect, log, or read your private transferred text, URLs, or clipboard snippets.

3. Payment Processing

Payments for premium features are handled securely via third-party payment gateways. We do not store sensitive financial details like credit card numbers or banking passwords on our servers.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage strictly to maintain user authentication tokens, theme preferences, and Zero-Knowledge session key parameters.

5. Contact Us

For privacy inquiries or account requests, please reach out to support@dropconnect.com.
