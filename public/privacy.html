Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how DropConnect collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect essential information required to provide our service, such as your email address, authentication provider credentials (Google Sign-In), and transaction details for subscription management.

2. Encryption & Data Security

All temporary clipboard data and live WebSocket transmissions are encrypted using client-side AES encryption. We do not inspect or read your private transferred snippets.

3. Third-Party Payment Processing

Payments are handled securely via Cashfree Payment Gateway. We do not store financial details like credit card numbers or banking passwords on our servers.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

We use local storage strictly to maintain user authentication tokens and application state.

5. Contact Us

For privacy queries, please reach out to support@dropconnect.com.
